FPS Victory Screen — Recognition & Progression

Victory screens that go beyond stats. This concept was built through pure rapid prototyping — no Figma, no design system — to explore how the moment could feel. Players earn points during the match (objectives, revives, support); at the end they spend them to recognize others, feeding long-term tracks like Medic, Objective, or Leadership.

The recognition layer draws from Overwatch endorsements, League honor, Valorant highlights, and Battlefield ribbons — combined into one results screen. Rapid sketching and prototyping first, then AI to turn the solid concept into flows, specs, and player journeys in hours.

Starship Diagnostic & Repair Console

A game interface concept that blends futuristic HUD design with functional systems. Subsystem grid (shields, engines, weapons, life support) with real-time status and one-click drill-down; immersive repair popups for resource planning and execution; damage analysis logs scrolling like a real ship’s console.

Sharp edges, bold colors, holographic effects, and 3D layering make it feel like working inside a starship’s command center. The goal: UI that feels part of the world, not a menu overlay — functional, interactive, and narratively consistent.

Magical Arsenal — Unified Inventory & Shop

A proof-of-concept UI where players manage, explore, and purchase items in one place. No separate shop: browse, filter by school, and buy directly from the inventory. Reduces friction, encourages exploration, and supports conversions.

Unified arsenal and shop; ownership and lock states for a clear progression loop; item showcase and lore for emotional value; immersive background so it feels part of the world. Good UI isn’t just looks — it’s systems that feel natural, rewarding, and worth investing in.

Fantasy RPG Loading Screen

Loading screens don’t have to be dead time. This concept pulls players into the experience before the game starts — gameplay tips, tactical info, and world lore in one psychologically tuned moment. HALO-Inspired Faction Selection

A faction selection interface for a sci-fi strategy or military sim. Players choose from 20 military factions (UNSC Marines, Covenant Elites, Forerunner Sentinels, etc.) via an infinite horizontal carousel with wraparound. Each faction shows roles, combat abilities, and tactical advantages; cinematic typing animations give a military-database feel.

Blue/cyan holographic styling, animated space background, semi-transparent panels, 25 specialization types with custom icons. No scrolling — compact, interactive, with hover glow and modal lore. Functional selection and immersive world-building in one.
